# Lead Conversion Skill — Ayusomam Herbals

## How to Convert a Sinus Lead to a Paying Customer

### Lead Funnel Stages

Every lead enters one of these stages:
- `new` → Chat started, no assessment done
- `assessment_complete` → Sinus type identified
- `contact_collected` → Name + phone captured
- `plans_shown` → Pricing shown
- `try_first` → Said they want to try free steps first
- `protocol_1_selected` → Chose Rs.499 plan
- `protocol_2_selected` → Chose Rs.1299 plan
- `member` → Payment confirmed

---

### Conversation Flow (5 Stages)

**STAGE 1 — UNDERSTAND**
Ask about: duration, main symptom, what they've tried, severity.
Never rush. ONE question at a time.

**STAGE 2 — IDENTIFY TYPE**
4 sinus types:
- `allergic` = Vataja-Kaphaja (sneezing, runny nose, dust triggers)
- `congestive` = Kaphaja (naak band, heavy face, morning worse)
- `heat` = Pittaja (burning, yellow mucus, headache)
- `dependency` = Dushta (can't breathe without nasal spray)

**STAGE 3 — EDUCATE**
Tell them WHY their type happens. Use the dosha explanation.
"Aapki nasal lining oversensitive ho gayi hai..."
Build authority before offering anything.

**STAGE 4 — FREE VALUE FIRST**
Give 2 immediate relief steps (morning or night based on time).
This builds trust. Most sales close after free steps.
Always end with: "Shaam ko zaroor batana — kuch fark aaya?"

**STAGE 5 — CLOSE**
After free steps, ask: "Kya aap structured protocol dekhna chahenge?"
Never mention price FIRST. Solve first, sell second.

### Objection Handling Scripts

**"Bahut mahanga hai"**
> "Ek ENT visit mein Rs.500-800 sirf consultation. Yahan Rs.499 mein 7 din ka poora protocol + daily guidance. Agar 7 din mein fark na aaye — toh baat kar sakte hain."

**"Pehle try karta hun"**
> "Bilkul, steps try karein. Subah aur raat dono. Kal batana kya fark aaya — main personally follow karunga/karungi."
(Set a follow-up reminder for next day.)

**"Sochna hai"**
> "Bilkul socho. Sirf ek cheez batao — sinus kitne saal se hai? 1 saal ya 3 saal? Agar 1+ saal hai toh foundation set ho raha hai andar — jitna time, utna deep hota hai."

**"Kya guarantee hai?"**
> "Guarantee nahi de sakte kyunki har body alag hoti hai. Lekin 500+ log try kar chuke hain — 7 din mein results dikhne lagte hain. Jo nahi dikhte woh andar ki condition ki baat karte hain openly."

**"Allopathy try karunga"**
> "Bilkul try karo. Agar aap already try kar chuke hain aur wapas aaya — toh aap jaante hain ki symptoms dab jaate hain, root cause waise ka waisa rehta hai."

### Lead Re-engagement (for `try_first` leads)

Follow up next day with:
> "[Name] Ji, kal ke steps try kiye? Kuch bhi fark aaya hoga — thoda bhi batao. Main samjhunga/samjhungi."

If no reply in 2 days:
> "[Name] Ji, sinus mein ek cheez hoti hai — jab thoda achha lagta hai, hum bhool jaate hain. Lekin root cause waise ka waisa rehta hai. Kya step karna hua?"

If no reply in 5 days:
> "Namaste [Name] Ji 🙏 Sachin Ji personally yaad kar rahe hain. Koi sawaal ho — reply karein. Ayusomam Herbals 🌿"

### Conversion Rate Benchmarks
- Chat opened → Assessment complete: 40-60%
- Assessment → Plans shown: 50-70%
- Plans shown → Payment: 10-20%
- Try first → Eventually converts: 30-40% (within 7 days with follow-up)

---

### Key Rules
1. Always use the customer's NAME after collecting it
2. Always validate first: "Haan, ye bahut uncomfortable hota hai"
3. Short messages: 2-4 lines WhatsApp style
4. Never use complex Ayurvedic terms in chat — use glossary substitutions
5. Always have a next step: either follow-up question or CTA
